I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the UC Berkeley School of Information affiliated with Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR), and am simultaneously receiving an M.S. in computer science from UC Berkeley EECS. I am co-advised by Dr. David Bamman and Dr. Ahmed Alaa, and am receiving a minor in Computational Precision Health from UCSF. My research interests are in:

  • Machine learning in medicine
  • Causal inference and time series
  • Human-centered AI
  • Clinical natural language processing

I received my master's degree from the University of Pennsylvania, and bachelor's degree from Claremont McKenna College. I interned with the Health Futures team at Microsoft Research (MSR) in the Summer of 2024.
