I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the UC Berkeley School of Information and Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR). I am pursuing an M.S. in Computer Science from UC Berkeley EECS, along with a minor in Computational Precision Health through a joint program with UCSF. I am co-advised by Dr. Ahmed Alaa and Dr. David Bamman.

My research develops and applies machine learning and statistical methods to medicine, with a focus on clinical natural language processing, time-series causal inference, and human-centered AI.

I received my master's degree from the University of Pennsylvania, and bachelor's degree from Claremont McKenna College. I previously interned with the Health Futures team at Microsoft Research (MSR).
