I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the University of California, Berkeley advised by Dr. David Bamman. My research interests are in machine learning for health, causal inference, and clinical natural language processing.

I am currently pursuing a designated emphasis in Computational Precision Health through U.C. Berkeley and U.C.S.F., and am a member of the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR), Bamman Group, and Alaa Lab. I received my master’s degree from the University of Pennsylvania, and bachelor’s degree from Claremont McKenna College.
