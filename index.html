I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the University of California, Berkeley advised by Dr. David Bamman. My research interests are in machine learning in medicine, causal inference, and clinical natural language processing.


I am currently pursuing a designated emphasis in Computational Precision Health through UC Berkeley and UCSF advised by Dr. Ahmed Alaa, and am a member of Berkeley AI Research (BAIR). I received my master’s degree from the University of Pennsylvania, and bachelor’s degree from Claremont McKenna College.
